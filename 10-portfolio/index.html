If you prefer prose

I started a tech support job so I could continue my college education. Customer support was hard but I loved untangling defects and that got the attention of a few people on our engineering team. They helped me up into quality assurance. I learned browser automation and pair programmed, was promoted to SDET. New project, new role: associate software engineer. I continue to grow and learn in the software engineering role, thriving on web dev somewhere between front-end and full-stack.
